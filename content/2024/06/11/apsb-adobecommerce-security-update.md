- **Priority:** 1
- **Severity:** Critical

- **Adobe Commerce:** 2.4.7 and earlier2.4.6-p5 and earlier2.4.5-p7 and earlier2.4.4-p8 and earlier2.4.3-ext-7 and earlier*2.4.2-ext-7 and earlier*
